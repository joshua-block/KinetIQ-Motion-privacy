Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 18, 2026

Introduction

KinetIQ Motion ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Motion and Sensor Data

KinetIQ Motion collects accelerometer and motion data from your Apple Watch during workout sessions. This includes:

  • Device acceleration measurements (X, Y, Z axes)
  • Gravity vector data for coordinate transformation
  • Timestamps of sensor readings

Workout Metadata

We collect information about your workouts, including:

  • Exercise type (e.g., squat, bench press, deadlift)
  • Weight used
  • Set numbers
  • Workout date and time

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • User authentication credentials (securely hashed)
  • Profile Photo (optional upload)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Calculate velocity, displacement, and performance metrics from your workout data
  • Store your workout history for review and analysis
  • Provide personalized training insights
  • Improve the accuracy of our motion tracking algorithms
  • Maintain and improve app functionality

Data Storage and Security

Your workout data is stored securely using Firebase Cloud Storage and Firebase Authentication. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Access controls limiting data access to your account only

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal workout data with third parties. Your data is private and belongs to you.

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) - for authentication and cloud storage. Firebase's privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your workout data at any time through the app
  • Delete your workout history
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your data (feature coming soon)

Children's Privacy

KinetIQ Motion is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Health and Fitness Disclaimer

KinetIQ Motion is a fitness tracking tool and is not a medical device. The app is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult with qualified fitness professionals and healthcare providers before beginning any exercise program.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention

We retain your workout data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.
